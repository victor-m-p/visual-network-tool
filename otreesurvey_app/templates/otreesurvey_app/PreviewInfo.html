What this study involves

This study consists of two parts:

  • Part 1 — Statement ratings. You will see a list of statements about meat-eating and be asked to rate how much you personally agree with each one, and how important it is to your own meat-eating behavior.
  • Part 2 — Short texts. You will read a few short texts containing arguments related to meat-eating. After each text you will be asked a couple of quick questions about it.
The full study should take no more than 10 minutes.
