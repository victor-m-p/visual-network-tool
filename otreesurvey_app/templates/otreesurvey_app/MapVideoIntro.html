What you will be doing in the next steps

  1. Place statements: You will drag the statements you created into a box and place things that "go together" closer to each other. For instance, place things that seem more similar or related to you closer together. Try to use the whole box for this task and avoid placing all statements on top of each other.
  2. Draw supporting connections between statements. For example if they reinforce or encourage each other. You will indicate how clear or strong each connection is on a scale. You can use high ratings for connections that feel very clear and strong to you, and low ratings for connections that you still feel but are more uncertain about.
  3. Draw conflicting connections between statements. For example if they undermine or contradict each other. You will indicate how clear or strong each connection is on a scale, as with the supporting connections.
