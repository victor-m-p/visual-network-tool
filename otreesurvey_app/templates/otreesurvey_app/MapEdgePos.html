How to connect circles

  1. Click a circle to select the statement.
  2. Click another circle to connect the statements.
  3. Indicate how strongly they support each other. The connection is created immediately when you select how strongly they support each other.
  4. Click the same pair again to remove the connection if you change your mind.

You can still move circles around if this helps you organize things.
